## The challenge

Blooms Academy wanted a single platform where students, teachers, and administrators
could manage the complete IELTS preparation journey — from practice to assessment to
live instruction.

## The approach

I built a multi-role platform that brings the whole journey into one place:
AI-assisted writing evaluation, speaking assessments, online courses, live classes,
and a capable admin content-management system.

## Highlights

- AI-assisted writing evaluation (Google Gemini)
- Timed exam engine for realistic practice
- Separate student and teacher dashboards
- Admin CMS for courses and content
- Razorpay payments and Zoom-based live classes
- OTP authentication

## Built with

React, Vite, Tailwind CSS, Node.js, Express, MongoDB, Google Gemini, and Razorpay.
