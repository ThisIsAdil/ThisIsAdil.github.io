## The challenge

Mavin Star needed a trustworthy online presence that encourages donations and
volunteer participation — without requiring backend infrastructure to maintain.

## The approach

I developed a lightweight, responsive website with custom UI components, structured
content, and clear donation and volunteer flows.

## Highlights

- Program showcase
- Clear donation and volunteer flows
- Custom carousel
- Responsive, fully static layout

## Built with

React, Vite, and React Router.
