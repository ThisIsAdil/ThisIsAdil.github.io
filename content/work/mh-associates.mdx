## The challenge

MH Associates needed to replace an outdated company presence with a professional
website focused on credibility and lead generation.

## The approach

I designed and developed a responsive marketing website with clear conversion paths,
reusable components, and an image-heavy gallery optimized for performance.

## Highlights

- Lead-generation-focused structure
- Responsive design across devices
- Services showcase and project gallery
- Downloadable company profile
- Floating contact widget

## Built with

React, Vite, and Tailwind CSS.
